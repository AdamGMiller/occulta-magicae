Occulta Magicae

Hints & Walkthrough

🕯 Getting Started

You begin with a handful of cards on a parchment table. The game runs in real time — seasons pass whether you act or not. Your goal is to master the Hermetic Arts and achieve one of eight victory conditions before the dangers overwhelm you.

Controls

  • Click a card to inspect it and see what it does
  • Drag a card onto a verb station (Study, Work, etc.) to use it
  • Click a verb slot to see which cards fit
  • Click a card in a slot to remove it (or drag it out)
  • Space to pause   123 to change speed   Esc to return to menu
  • L to open the Chronicle (message log)
  • Scroll to zoom, drag empty space to pan
  • Use the layout buttons (top right) to switch between grid, column, and compact views

Your First Turn

  1. Pause the game (Space) and read your cards
  2. Drag A Tattered Folio onto Study — this teaches you Vim (raw magic)
  3. Drag Ars Creationis onto Study — this teaches you Creo (creation)
  4. Drag A Letter from a Stranger onto Study — this introduces you to a Magus of the Order, unlocking the Speak verb
  5. Drag A Wandering Magus onto Speak — he teaches you Intellego (perception), unlocking the Explore verb
  6. Study vis to gain warping — at warping 2, the Dream verb unlocks
  7. Keep studying to reach Art level 3 — this unlocks the Work verb

It takes several seasons to unlock all verbs. Be patient — the early game is about building your foundation.

📖 How the Verbs Work

  • Study — Drag texts, vis, or art fragments here. Studying a text gives you a new Art. Studying two matching Art fragments deepens that Art (e.g., two Vim Level 1 → one Vim Level 2). Study your Fervor for random Art insight (costs hubris). Study your Acumen to reduce Hubris.
  • Work (unlocks at Art level 3) — Drag a Technique (Creo, Perdo, etc.) and a Form (Vim, Ignem, etc.) to craft a spell. Cast spells for various effects.
  • Dream (unlocks at warping 2) — Drag a Vim art to enter the Twilight (gives Enigma mystery cards, risky). Drag a Mystery card to advance that path. Drag Vigor to rest and reduce Corruption (slowly, may gain warping).
  • Explore (unlocks with Intellego) — Drag an Intellego art to discover new locations. Drag a location to search it for vis, texts, and secrets.
  • Speak (unlocks when you meet a Magus) — Drag companions to learn and trade. Drag Silver to reduce Notoriety.
  • Time Passes — Automatic. Each season grants silver, sometimes vis, and eventually companions. Threats may appear.

⚠ Threats

Threats appear as red-bordered stations alongside your verbs. When a threat spawns, it captures one of your cards — the villagers take your silver, the priest confiscates a text, warping absorbs your vis. You can see which card was captured.

To resolve a threat, drag the required card type into the threat's slot. If you resolve it in time, you get your captured card back. If the timer expires, the captured card is lost and worse effects follow.

⚠ The Four Dangers

Four status bars track your peril. If any reaches 10, you lose:

  • Warping — Accumulates from powerful magic. At 10: consumed by Twilight. Reduced by mastering the Enigma.
  • Notoriety — The mundane world's attention. At 10: the Inquisition. Reduced by spending Silver in Speak, or casting Veil of Invisibility.
  • Corruption — Infernal taint from dark places. At 10: damnation. Reduced by resting (Dream + Vigor) or casting Demon's Eternal Oblivion.
  • Hubris — Magical overreach. At 10: lost to obsession. Reduced by Study + Acumen (clears the mind).

🏆 Victory Conditions

There are eight paths to victory. Each requires a different approach.

✧ Ascension Through Twilight

Difficulty: Medium

Complete the Enigma of Criamon mystery (all 4 steps), reach Vim level 5, and have Warping ≥ 8.

Strategy: Dream + Vim art to enter Twilight and advance Enigma. Study vis and texts to push Vim to 5. Let warping accumulate — you need it. The Enigma protects you from Twilight loss.

⚙ Archmagus

Difficulty: Hard

Master 2 Techniques to level 4+ and 2 Forms to level 4+.

Strategy: Study aggressively — every text, every vis. Speak with the Magus for new arts (up to 4 times reliably). Explore for texts. Combine matching art fragments in Study to level up. Watch Hubris — use Acumen regularly.

❋ Hierophant of the Mysteries

Difficulty: Hard

Complete 3 of the 9 mystery paths to step 4.

Strategy: Study Animal ≥2 for Heartbeast. Study Terram ≥2 for Artifice. Dream + Vim for Enigma. Explore Silver Wood for Arcadia. Then Dream with each mystery card to advance. Hubris grows with each mystery step — manage it with Acumen.

🏰 Covenant Primus

Difficulty: Medium

Have the Aegis of the Hearth spell + 3 companions + 2 locations + 3 texts.

Strategy: Build resources. Craft Aegis of the Hearth (Work: Rego + Vim). Collect companions from time events (seasons 3, 6, 14). Explore with Intellego for locations. Keep texts by not studying all of them. A builder's victory.

🔨 The Great Working

Difficulty: Very Hard

Complete the Artifice mystery path + have 5 unique items.

Strategy: Study Terram ≥2 to start Artifice. Dream with Artifice mystery cards. Then Work with spells and vis to create enchanted items. Some items come from mystery rewards. The Artificer's path.

🎵 The Hymn Primordial

Difficulty: Hard

Complete the Hyperborean Hymns mystery path + Warping ≥ 5.

Strategy: The Hymns path triggers from studying Imaginem art. Dream with the mystery cards to advance. Warping accumulates from magic use. A path of dangerous beauty.

🕊 Peacemaker of the Order

Difficulty: Very Hard

Resolve 8+ threats + have 3 companions + keep Notoriety ≤ 2.

Strategy: Never ignore threats — resolve every one by dragging the right card into the threat slot. Spend silver to keep notoriety low. Collect companions. This requires patience and resource management over many seasons.

🌀 The Forbidden Synthesis

Difficulty: Very Hard

Hold cards from all four realms (Magic, Divine, Faerie, Infernal) + complete 1 mystery path.

Strategy: Magic cards are common. Faerie cards come from Silver Wood. Infernal cards come from Ruined Chapel. Divine cards come from rare companions. Complete any mystery path. A dangerous balancing act — corruption rises from Infernal cards.

🗝 Mystery Paths

There are nine mystery paths, each triggered differently:

  • Enigma of Criamon — Triggered by Dream + Vim art (entering Twilight). The Twilight itself advances this path.
  • Heartbeast of Bjornaer — Triggered by studying Animal to level 2+. Then Dream with the mystery cards.
  • Verdant Artifice — Triggered by studying Terram to level 2+. Then Dream with the mystery cards.
  • Path of Arcadia — Triggered by exploring Faerie locations (The Silver Wood). Then Dream with the mystery cards.
  • Path of Necromancy — Triggered by studying Corpus to level 3+ or exploring dark locations. Then Dream with mystery cards.
  • Path of the Runes — Triggered by discovering ancient Scandinavian texts. Then Dream with mystery cards.
  • Hyperborean Hymns — Triggered by studying Imaginem to level 2+. Then Dream with mystery cards.
  • Path of the Body — Branched from Enigma at higher levels. Then Dream with mystery cards.
  • Path of Strife — Branched from Enigma at higher levels. A dark path with powerful rewards.

💡 Tips

  • Pause often! Read your cards. The game rewards understanding over speed.
  • Click verb slots to see what fits — the gold glow shows compatible cards.
  • Threats steal your cards! Resolve them quickly by dragging the right card into the threat's slot. If the timer expires, you lose the captured card and suffer worse effects.
  • A guaranteed threat appears at season 2 — be ready with silver.
  • The Wandering Magus teaches reliably for 4 visits, then becomes less helpful. Make each visit count.
  • Silver Pennies are vital — for threats, bribes, and the Tax Collector. Don't spend them all.
  • Clearing corruption with Dream+Vigor is slow (−1 per rest) and may cause warping. Prevention is better than cure.
  • Hubris clears slowly with Study+Acumen (−1). Don't let it build up past 7.
  • The Ruined Chapel is dangerous (infernal taint). The Silver Wood is rewarding (faerie). Choose wisely.
  • Study your Fervor for a random Art insight — useful when you're stuck but it raises Hubris.
  • When dragging cards, watch for red ✕ on slots — this means that verb can't complete even if you place your card there.
  • You can remove a card from a verb slot by clicking it or dragging it out (only before the verb starts processing).

⚔ Loss Conditions

If any danger stat reaches 10, you lose:

  • Warping 10 — Consumed by Twilight (unless you've completed the Enigma → then it's Ascension)
  • Notoriety 10 — The Inquisition
  • Corruption 10 — Damned by the Infernal
  • Hubris 10 — Lost to Obsession
